**Problem:**
Acrobat crashes a few seconds after opening a PDF.

**Cause:**
the acrobat updater...

**Solution:**
Go to acrobat plug-in directory (`c:\program files\adobe\acrobat 8.0\acrobat\plug_ins`) and rename `updater.api` to something else.
